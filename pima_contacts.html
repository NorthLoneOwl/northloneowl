Pima County Board of Supervisors — How and When to Contact

How and When to Get in Touch With the County Board of Supervisors.

Which District is the Proposed Airstrip in?
The proposed airstrip is in District 3, but the whole board will vote on the project.
It would be most helpful to message everyone on the board with concerns or opinions.

How Best to Make Your Voice Heard?
Here is the best link to send comments: Pima County Webform. A message from there goes to the Clerk of the Board and hence to all the Supervisors. Once there is an official case number, you can connect it directly in your message so that your message gets grouped with all the information for the case, and so your message does not get lost.

When is the Best Time to Write to Make Your Voice Heard?
You can write anytime to raise concerns and get information infront of the Board of Supervisors.
However, for the comments to become an official part of the Planning and Zoning Commissions’s record, they need to be submitted once there is an official case number. If you are already on our email list or check our Facebook page, we will update you as soon as this happens. Not sure you're on our list? Just email us NorthLoneOwl@gmail.com
